MEET OUR FOUNDERS

Tawiah Elijah is a passionate philanthropist from the Greater Accra Region of Ghana, dedicated to empowering communities through sustainable development and education. His journey began at Doryumu Methodist Basic School and continued through Aveyime Battor Senior High School. Fueled by his commitment to clean energy and social impact, he pursued Renewable Energy Engineering at the University of Mines and Technology (UMaT) and is currently completing his degree at the University of Environment and Sustainable Development in Somanya.

Inspired by the same values that guide our organization, Elijah leads a passionate team committed to creating lasting change in communities that need it most. Through compassion, collaboration, and action, we work tirelessly to support vulnerable populations, promote dignity, and uplift lives. Tawiah Job is a dynamic Business Administration graduate from the University of Professional Studies, Accra (UPSA), with a versatile background in logistics supervision, human resource administration, sales, and data management. As a detail-oriented problem solver and team leader, he has successfully managed operations such as supervising over 10 escort officers at Icon Energy and overseeing HR and stock reconciliation processes with precision.

Job also brings an entrepreneurial mindset through his ventures in fish farming and real estate, showcasing his drive for sustainable development and economic empowerment. With his strong leadership skills, analytical thinking, and passion for community growth, he is a valuable stakeholder for any foundation focused on impactful development, operational efficiency, and social transformation.

Angela Jahn is a talented fashion designer and the creative force behind Styles and Colours to Fit Fashion, a brand that specializes in bridal alterations, bespoke womenswear, and garment repairs. With an artistic eye and a passion for detail, she turns every piece of fabric into a story of elegance and self-expression.

Angela currently serves as a Content Creator for New International Church, where she produces visual stories that inspire and engage. Her creative skill set spans across fashion design, graphic design (tabletop), and social media storytelling. Multilingual in English and Ewe, and currently learning German (A1), Angela is a culturally fluent communicator and a strong advocate for using creative platforms to empower communities.
